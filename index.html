    • What is MusicSpace?

    The main concern of the ever improving technology in this era is the comfort provided to the user in using the tools and technologies without worrying and without too much hassle in doing his/her task .It is always easy when things are available in a single place. And music is considered very soothing and comforting. This application concentrate on solving the problem of finding and booking music bands and solo artists for various events and occasions .In our country , generally people just fit some speakers and play songs online which becomes boring and repetitive due to lack of availability of authentic artists or bands .




    How does the MusicSpace work?

    With this application an user will be able register , search and book various music bands or artists for any event such as wedding , birthday ,office party, annual functions , etc. as well as register themselves as an artist if they have the talent . The bands or artists have to register themselves and provide their information such as name of the artist/band ,number of members and their names , music type in which they specialize, event preference ,charges per event contact, past performances and reviews ,etc. which will all be up to date .These all information will be shown to the booker while booking the band so that he can thoroughly check the band according to his musical preference .After booking the band the user can directly contact the band with their provided contact or use the application as an mediator for communication. After the successful completion of the event the user can give ratings and reviews for the performance for future preferences and recommendations. This all will help the society to expose themselves to music and will also help the music industry to find some new and genuine music artists.
